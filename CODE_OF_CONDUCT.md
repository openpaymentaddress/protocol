# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive community. Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, threats, and bad-faith disruption are not acceptable.

Report conduct concerns privately to [opap@patricksavalle.com](mailto:opap@patricksavalle.com). Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or take other proportionate action to protect the community.

By participating in this repository, you agree to uphold these standards.
